Plastic Regiment

Note: Currently only the Windows version is available. A HTML5 version is in the works.

Destroy the oppositing plastic regiment's toy box, and conquer the room!

Controls:
WASD - Move
Space - Jump
Mouse - Aim
Left Mouse - Fire
1-4 - Switch Weapons
